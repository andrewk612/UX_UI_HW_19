About Me

Hi, I’m Andrew Kellogg. I am deeply passionate about design in all forms, especially UX UI. Coming from a graphic design and music background, creativity has always been a strength of mine and something I really enjoy implementing in my work, whatever the work might be. I use my empathy to build great experiences for people through design.

My skills include user research & analysis, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, graphic design, layout design, presentation, and project management.

I use Figma for visualization and easy collaboration, and then code in HTML, CSS and Javascript to bring the designs to life.
